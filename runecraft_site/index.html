Who we are

Two childhood players, one gargantuan idea.

We're Marc and David, two childhood friends who grew up on Runescape and Minecraft.

Our goal is simple. Recreate Gielinor in the immersive world only Minecraft can provide, in the breathtaking detail that Conquest Reforged offers.

It's our biggest undertaking, but we're itching to get started.

2 builders 0 hours time invested to date 100% fan-made

The modpack

Built with Conquest Reforged

Conquest Reforged is an aesthetic Minecraft mod which was developed for players looking to push their builds far beyond the limitations of the base game. With over 15,000 blocks to choose from, you can achieve realism well beyond the possibilities of Vanilla.
